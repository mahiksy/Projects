LET'S SEE WHO IS THE WINNER!







DESCRIPTION

the purpose of this game is to guess the distance in a way that car gets to the passenger. the sizes of the highway, cars and passnegers are 1000px,90px, and 20px respectivly.

the player wins tha game if the car stops in a way that passenger has access to the car's door.

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Click on the passenger botton to place the passenger on the gameboard
  2. Players put their distance numbers from 0 to 1000 and click on Enter
  3. click on Start to start the game
  4. click on Refresh to restart the game
